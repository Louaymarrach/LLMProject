Title: Schools, Departments, and Academic Programs

LAU is structured into seven academic schools offering over 60 undergraduate and graduate degrees:

1. **School of Arts & Sciences:**
   - Departments: Computer Science, Communication Arts, English, Humanities, Mathematics & Physics, Natural Sciences, Social Sciences.
   - Programs: BA in Communication Arts, BS in Computer Science, BS in Biology, MA in Education, MA in International Affairs, and more.

2. **School of Engineering:**
   - Programs: BE in Computer, Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, and Industrial Engineering; MS in Engineering Management; ME in Civil Engineering.

3. **Adnan Kassar School of Business:**
   - Programs: B.S. in Business, Economics, Accounting, and Hospitality; MBA and Executive MBA.
   - AACSB accredited.

4. **School of Architecture & Design:**
   - Programs: BArch, BFA in Graphic Design, Interior Design, and Fashion Design.

5. **Alice Ramez Chagoury School of Nursing:**
   - BS in Nursing (CCNE-accredited) and MSN.

6. **Gilbert & Rose-Marie Chagoury School of Medicine:**
   - MD program, affiliated clinical centers, and research facilities.

7. **School of Pharmacy:**
   - BS in Pharmacy and PharmD (ACPE-accredited).
