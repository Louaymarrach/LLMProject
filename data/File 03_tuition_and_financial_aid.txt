Title: Tuition, Scholarships, and Financial Aid at LAU (2024–2025)

Tuition Fees:
Tuition is calculated on a per-credit basis and varies by program:
- Undergraduate: $700–$900 per credit
- Graduate: $800–$1,000 per credit
- Medicine and Pharmacy: Fixed annual tuition rates.

Additional fees include registration, laboratory use, technology, and activity charges. Payments can be made in USD or LBP equivalent at the market rate.

Financial Aid:
Over 40% of LAU students receive financial support through need-based aid, scholarships, and on-campus employment. The Financial Aid Office offers:
- Merit-Based Scholarships: Awarded to students with exceptional academic performance (usually 3.5 GPA or higher).
- Need-Based Grants: Determined by family income, assets, and number of dependents.
- Endowed Scholarships: Funded by alumni and donors.
- Graduate Assistantships: Partial tuition waivers and stipends for research/teaching assistance.
- Work-Study Program: Part-time campus jobs (library, labs, offices).

Applicants for aid must submit an application through the Financial Aid Portal with income documentation.
