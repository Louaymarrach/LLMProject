Title: Student Life, Clubs & Extracurricular Activities

Overview:
LAU offers an active campus life with more than 80 student clubs, sports teams, and cultural organizations.

Common clubs & organizations:
- Engineering Society
- Computer Science Club
- Debate Club
- Music and Arts Society
- Red Cross Youth Club
- Entrepreneurship Club
- Environmental Club
- Model United Nations (LAU MUN)

Sports and recreation:
- LAU Athletics Department manages basketball, football, volleyball, swimming, and fitness programs.
- Access to gym facilities and sports events.

Student Government:
- Each campus has elected student councils that coordinate events and represent the student body.

Events:
- Annual Spring Fair, International Day, Career Fair, Arts Festival.
