Title: LAU Registration, Portal, and Academic Advising

All students use the LAU Student Information System (SIS) at https://sis.lau.edu.lb for registration, tuition payment, and academic tracking.

Registration Steps:
1. Log in to SIS using your LAU credentials.
2. Review your Degree Audit under “Advising.”
3. Meet your assigned academic advisor to discuss your semester plan.
4. Register during your allocated time slot.
5. Confirm and pay tuition to finalize registration.

The portal also provides:
- Course Catalog and Degree Audit
- Grade Reports and GPA Calculation
- Financial Statements and Invoices
- Access to Blackboard (learning.lau.edu.lb)

Late registration, add/drop, and withdrawal deadlines are listed in the Academic Calendar.
