Title: Research, Labs & Innovation at LAU

Overview:
LAU encourages student and faculty research through university-funded initiatives, collaborations, and research centers.

Major centers & initiatives:
- LAU School of Engineering Research Labs (Robotics, Embedded Systems, Civil Materials)
- Gilbert and Rose-Marie Chagoury School of Medicine Research Center
- LAU Artificial Intelligence & Data Science Group
- Center for Lebanese Heritage
- Institute for Social Justice and Conflict Resolution (ISJCR)

Undergraduate research opportunities:
- Many departments offer capstone projects and internships integrated with ongoing research.

Funding & support:
- Internal research grants, assistantships, and collaborations with local industries.
