Title: Campus Life & Extracurricular Activities at LAU

LAU provides a vibrant campus life that encourages student engagement beyond academics. Students can participate in numerous clubs, student organizations, and leadership programs across both Beirut and Byblos campuses.

Key components of campus life include:
- Student Clubs: Academic, cultural, social, and hobby-based clubs for networking and personal growth.
- Sports & Athletics: LAU supports intramural and inter-university competitions, with facilities for basketball, football, volleyball, swimming, and fitness centers.
- Cultural Events: Regular events, lectures, workshops, and art exhibitions organized to promote cultural exchange and creativity.
- Student Government: Opportunities for student representation and participation in university decision-making.
- Volunteering & Community Service: Programs for students to engage with local communities, NGOs, and social projects.
- Orientation Programs: Activities designed to help new students integrate and familiarize themselves with campus facilities and academic expectations.

These activities aim to create a balanced student experience, fostering personal development, leadership, and lifelong skills alongside academic success.
