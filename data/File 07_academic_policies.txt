Title: Academic Rules, Standing, and Integrity

Grading System (4.0 scale):
A = 4.0 (Excellent)  
B+ = 3.3  
B = 3.0 (Good)  
C+ = 2.3  
C = 2.0 (Satisfactory)  
D = 1.0 (Pass)  
F = 0.0 (Fail)

Academic Standing:
- Minimum cumulative GPA: 2.0 for good standing.

- Repeated probation may lead to suspension or dismissal.

Attendance:
Students must attend 80% of classes. Absences beyond this may result in withdrawal or failure.

Integrity and Ethics:
Plagiarism, cheating, or falsification of documents are treated as serious offenses and handled by the Academic Integrity Committee.

Grade Appeal Procedure:
Students can appeal within 2 weeks of grade posting by submitting a formal request to the department chair.
