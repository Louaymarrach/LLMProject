Title: LAU Housing, Accommodation, and Transportation

Housing:
- Available primarily at Byblos Campus.
- Options: Single, double, and triple rooms with private or shared facilities.
- Amenities: Wi-Fi, security, 24-hour assistance, study lounges, kitchens, laundry rooms, and recreational areas.
- Applications are made online via the Housing Portal before semester start.

Transportation:
- Regular LAU shuttle service between Beirut and Byblos.
- Designated pickup points in Beirut and Jounieh.
- Many students also use ride-sharing or private vans.

Off-Campus Housing:
- Recommended nearby areas: Blat, Jbeil, and Halat.
- LAU assists with finding off-campus housing through its Housing Office.
