Title: Student Life, Support, and Services

Student Support Offices:
- **Office of Student Development & Enrollment Management (SDEM):** Organizes orientation, student engagement, and clubs.
- **Counseling Center:** Provides academic and psychological counseling sessions.
- **Career Guidance Office:** Helps with internships, CV writing, and job placement.
- **Health Services:** On-campus medical units with certified nurses and physicians.
- **IT Services:** Technical support, account management, and Wi-Fi assistance.

Other Campus Services:
- Sports and fitness facilities, gyms, and swimming pools.
- Clubs and societies (Robotics Club, Music Club, Debate Society, etc.).
- Dining halls and cafes on both campuses.
