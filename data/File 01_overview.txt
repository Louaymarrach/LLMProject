Title: Lebanese American University (LAU) Overview

The Lebanese American University (LAU) is one of the leading higher education institutions in Lebanon and the Middle East. Established in 1924, it operates as a private, nonsectarian university with two main campuses located in Beirut and Byblos. LAU provides an American-style education emphasizing academic excellence, liberal arts foundations, civic engagement, and innovation.

LAU is accredited by the New England Commission of Higher Education (NECHE), ensuring that its degrees are globally recognized. It also maintains specialized accreditations from international bodies such as ABET (for Engineering and Computer Science programs), AACSB (for Business), and CCNE (for Nursing).
