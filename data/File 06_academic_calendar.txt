Title: Academic Calendar and Important Deadlines

LAU follows the semester system with Fall, Spring, and Summer terms. The Academic Calendar outlines key academic events:

- **Fall Semester (September – December):**
  - Orientation: First week of September
  - Add/Drop: First 2 weeks of classes
  - Midterms: October
  - Final Exams: Mid-December

- **Spring Semester (February – May):**
  - Registration: January
  - Midterms: March
  - Finals: May

- **Summer Term (June – August):**
  - Short 6–8 week courses for selected subjects.

Other Key Dates:
- National Holidays (e.g., Independence Day, Labor Day)
- Graduation Ceremonies in June
- Deadlines for tuition payment, withdrawal, and incomplete grade resolution.
