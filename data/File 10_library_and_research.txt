Title: Libraries, Research, and Innovation at LAU

LAU Libraries System:
- Located in both Beirut and Byblos campuses.
- Provide access to 200,000+ books, 80+ online databases, and 40,000+ e-journals.
- Digital catalog: https://library.lau.edu.lb

Key Features:
- Research support and citation workshops.
- Interlibrary loan services and e-reserve materials.
- Access to major databases: IEEE, JSTOR, ScienceDirect, ACM, EBSCOhost, and ProQuest.
